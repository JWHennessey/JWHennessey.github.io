About Me

I'm currently a computer vision research engineer at Pupil. I was a doctoral student at University College London in the Smart Geometry Processing Group under the supervision of Prof. Niloy J. Mitra. My graduate work was at the intersection of computer graphics, geometry processing and human-computer interaction.

Previously I was an MSc Computer Science student at UCL, I graduated top of my class and was awarded the Peter Williams Prize. I also hold a BA from the University of Leeds.
